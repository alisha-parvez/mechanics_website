Step 2

Drawing P,V and M diagrams
for point loads and moments

The next part of learning beam statics is learning how to draw beam statics diagrams for simple loads (of one type). We start with drawing the P,V and M diagrams for point loads and moments.
The values of the shear force, axial force and bending moment at any point (distance: x) is found by passing a cut at that point and applying the equilibrium equations on the sections obtained.
