Concept for ex 1

All the bodies we deal with in this chapter are in equilibrium. In order to be in equilibrium, the sum of all the forces on the body must be zero.
Thus, we assign two mutually perpendicular directions as positive x and y (as shown)
Then, we apply the equations
Fx=0 and Fy=0

On applying the equations for the given diagram, we get: (Note: The values Fx1, Fy1, etc denote the magnitude only)

  • Fx1 - Fx2 = 0
  • -Fy1 - Fy2 + Fy3 + Fy4 + Fy5 = 0
